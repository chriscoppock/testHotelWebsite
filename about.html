About Hotel BT

Hotel BT is a modern boutique hotel in Washington DC. Situated within walking distance to the Kennedy Center of Performing Arts, George Washington University & Hospital, The National Academy of Science, The National Mall, DC Metro, Government Agencies, World Bank, trendy shopping, dining, & nightlife of Foggy Bottom and Georgetown.
